About and Features

Our Story

After winning the Washington State Lottery in 2015, Bernie Black Bear was finally able to realize his dream of providing accommodations and amenities for rest and relaxation to animals from all over the world.

Bernie teamed up with his long-time travel companion Harvey Hippo, who provides the business savvy to make this venture both practical and sustainable.

Features

  • Hunting Grounds for Carnivores. Had your fill of the buffet? Enter our premium hunting grounds - grass fed and antibiotic free prey!

  • Safe Zones for Herbivores. Want to roam without worrying about that pesky predator? Enter our mediation savanna and roam to your heart's content. Pesticide free grasslands!

  • Casino Royale. Swim up slot machines for water based guests!

  • Entertainment Venues. See top mammalian performers and reptilian DJs!

  • Duty-free Shopping and Fitness Center.

RESORT CONDUCT POLICY: To help ensure that all guests are able to enjoy a safe and healthy vacation, they are required to follow our resort conduct policy through their stay. Guests are not allowed to eat other guests. Hunting is restricted to the Hunting Grounds for Carnivores (additional fee and safety deposit required).
